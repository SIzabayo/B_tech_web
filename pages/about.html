About RP Ngoma College

Empowering students with skills, knowledge, and opportunities.

Who We Are

RP Ngoma College is one of Rwanda’s leading institutions specializing in technical, vocational, and ICT-driven education. We are committed to delivering excellence through advanced training, modern facilities, and experienced staff dedicated to shaping future innovators.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable, and industry-aligned education that equips students with professional and technical skills needed for success.

Our Vision

To become a center of excellence in delivering modern and practical education that empowers future leaders and innovators in Rwanda and beyond.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical standards in all our activities.

Excellence

We strive for academic and professional excellence in everything we do.

Innovation

We encourage creativity and modern solutions for real-world challenges.

Community

We support teamwork, respect, and a shared sense of responsibility.

Our Journey

2015

RP Ngoma College was officially established to provide technical training.

2018

Launched ICT and Business Management programs.

2021

Expanded facilities with new labs and digital learning equipment.

2024

Recognized among Rwanda’s top institutions for technical education.
